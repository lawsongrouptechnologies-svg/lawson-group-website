About

You've heard the AI pitch. Here's the AI delivery record.

Lawson Group exists because most businesses don't need an AI strategy. They need someone who has actually shipped AI systems people use, and will do it for them without enterprise-consulting overhead.

The person behind it

Jeremy Sheriff

For six and a half years, Jeremy worked at Capco, one of the world's top financial services consultancies, on enterprise implementation for Fortune 100 banks, insurers, and asset managers. He spent the last three of those years leading GenAI implementation. These are environments where an AI mistake is not an inconvenience; it is a regulatory event.

What those years taught him: the technology is rarely the hard part. The hard part is structuring what a business actually knows so AI can use it reliably. That discipline, knowledge architecture first and automation second, is the core of every Lawson Group engagement.
