The problem

Every business runs on tribal knowledge

Your processes, your judgment calls, the way you quote a job or answer the question customers always ask — it's all undocumented. When the person who knows is out sick, busy, or gone, the business slows down. AI was supposed to fix this. For most companies, it became another tool nobody quite uses.

Most AI projects fail not because of the technology — but because nobody structured the knowledge underneath it.
