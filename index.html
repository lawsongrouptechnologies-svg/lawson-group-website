The problem

Every business runs on tribal knowledge

Your processes, your judgment calls, the way you quote a job or answer the question customers always ask — it's all undocumented. When the person who knows is out sick, busy, or gone, the business slows down. AI was supposed to fix this. For most companies, it became another tool nobody quite uses.

Most AI projects fail not because of the technology — but because nobody structured the knowledge underneath it.

The solution

The Company Brain

A structured, AI-powered operating layer built on tools you already use. Your knowledge, organized and searchable. Your processes, documented and automated. Your team, answering questions once instead of forever.

Structured knowledge

Institutional memory captured as living documents your AI — and your people — can actually use.

Workflow automation

Lead capture, follow-ups, daily digests, reporting — connected to your existing email, calendar, and CRM.

Working systems, day one

You get a system your team uses immediately. Not a roadmap. Not a strategy deck.
